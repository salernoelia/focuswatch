# Contribution Guidelines


## Rules

- Always use branches (features, bugs)
- Formulate a short and concise pull-request, reference the according Issue
- You are allowed to accept your own pull-request, but preferably involve a second party for validation


## Getting Started

After cloning the repository, make sure to add a developer team in the XCode signing tab.
The `Example.xcconfig` shall be duplicated and renamed to `Development.xcconfig`.
